Commissioners for Oaths

Commissioner for Oaths Services

Looking for a Commissioner for Oaths to authorise statements and verify legal documents, including affidavits? Call us at MI Law for a prompt and cost-effective service at our office in Luton.

You might need our services as Commissioners for Oaths if you need to provide any one of the following for a court proceeding:

  • Affirmation
  • Declaration
  • Acknowledgement
  • Examination
  • Attestation
  • Certification of documents

What does a Commissioner for Oaths do?

We make various checks that will be accepted by a court including:

  1. Making sure evidence required is in writing (this is a draft affidavit)
  2. Ensure that you have read your affidavit and understand what it contains
  3. Require you to swear that the affidavit is true under oath
  4. To verify that you swore the oath correctly

A fee will be charged for the above services.

You will also be required to bring two forms of identity with you:

  1. Photo identity
  2. Proof of your address valid within the last three months
